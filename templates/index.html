However, the price you paid at the time of purchase will remain valid for you. For more details, please refer to the information available on TechGraspers’ website.

Refund Policy

TechGraspers reserves the right to postpone or cancel any event, class, session, or internship, or to change the event location due to reasons such as insufficient enrollment, unforeseen presenter absences, or circumstances like floods, earthquakes, political instability, etc. In the event of a cancellation, you will be notified before the start date and will be offered either full credit towards a future event or a complete refund. Please allow up to 30 days from the cancellation notice for refunds to be processed. We recommend consulting a TechGraspers representative before making any travel arrangements for workshops, events, classes, sessions, or internships.

CANCELLATIONS BY THE PARTICIPANT

Please note that once the fees are paid, they are non-refundable under any circumstances.

CANCELLATIONS BY THE TECHGRASPERS

If TechGraspers needs to cancel an event/Session/Internship, you will be notified by phone or email before the scheduled start date. In such cases, you will receive a full 100% refund of the course fee paid by the candidate. Please allow up to 30 days for the refund process. This policy also applies to any partial or installment payments made for courses, sessions, or internships.

RESCHEDULES BY THE PARTICIPANT

All rescheduling requests must be submitted via email, and the following rules will apply:

  • More than 15 days before the workshop/session/internship: Credit will be issued for a future workshop.
  • Less than 15 days before the workshop: Credit will be issued for a future workshop/session/internship, along with a 10% rescheduling fee based on the standard course fee.

Please note that all cancellations will incur a 5% deduction from the refunded amount to cover transactional costs. This deduction does not include any transactional fees incurred by the customer during the initial online payment
